Soup Kitchen Task List

Important tasks and responsibilities for soup kitchen volunteers

Food Preparation

  • Wash and chop vegetables
  • Prepare salads and side dishes
  • Cook soups and main dishes
  • Portion food for serving
  • Prepare desserts and beverages

Serving

  • Set up serving stations
  • Serve food to guests
  • Refill serving trays as needed
  • Assist guests with special dietary needs
  • Provide water and beverages

Cleaning

  • Clear and wipe tables
  • Wash dishes and utensils
  • Clean kitchen equipment
  • Sweep and mop floors
  • Take out trash and recycling
  • Sanitize food preparation surfaces

Guest Relations

  • Welcome guests
  • Assist with seating
  • Engage in friendly conversation
  • Provide information about other services
  • Collect feedback about meals

Administrative

  • Track number of meals served
  • Maintain inventory of supplies
  • Coordinate with food donors
  • Assist with volunteer scheduling
  • Document any incidents or concerns

Volunteer Guidelines

Please arrive 30 minutes before your scheduled shift to receive instructions and prepare for service.

Wear closed-toe shoes and comfortable clothing. Aprons will be provided.

Wash hands thoroughly before handling food and wear gloves when serving.

If you are unable to fulfill your commitment, please notify us as soon as possible so we can find a replacement.

Thank you for your dedication to serving our community!
