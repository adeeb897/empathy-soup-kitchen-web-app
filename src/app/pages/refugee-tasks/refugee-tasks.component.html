Refugee Support Task List

Important tasks and responsibilities for refugee support volunteers

Welcome & Orientation

  • Greet new arrivals at airports or bus stations
  • Provide orientation to local community
  • Help with initial housing setup
  • Explain public transportation options
  • Introduce local shopping options

Language Support

  • Provide basic English language tutoring
  • Assist with translation at appointments
  • Help with filling out forms and applications
  • Create visual guides for common tasks
  • Connect refugees with language learning resources

Employment Assistance

  • Help with resume creation
  • Assist with job applications
  • Provide interview preparation
  • Connect with potential employers
  • Explain local work culture and expectations

Healthcare Navigation

  • Schedule medical appointments
  • Provide transportation to healthcare facilities
  • Explain healthcare system and insurance
  • Assist with medication management
  • Connect with mental health resources

Education Support

  • Help with school enrollment
  • Assist with homework and tutoring
  • Connect with educational resources
  • Explain school system and expectations
  • Facilitate parent-teacher communication

Community Integration

  • Introduce to community events and activities
  • Connect with cultural or religious communities
  • Organize social gatherings
  • Provide cultural orientation
  • Facilitate friendships with locals

Volunteer Guidelines

Cultural sensitivity is essential. Take time to learn about the cultural backgrounds of the refugees you are supporting.

Be patient and understanding. Many refugees have experienced trauma and face significant challenges adjusting to a new environment.

Respect boundaries and privacy. Allow refugees to share their stories at their own pace.

Focus on empowerment rather than dependency. The goal is to help refugees become self-sufficient.

Coordinate with our refugee services coordinator to ensure consistent support and avoid duplication of efforts.

Thank you for your compassion and dedication to supporting refugees in our community!
