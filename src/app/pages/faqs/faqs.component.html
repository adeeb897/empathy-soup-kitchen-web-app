Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our services

When are you open?

The soup kitchen opens every weekend (Saturday and Sunday) at 1:00 pm and serves until 2:00 pm. You are welcome to arrive anytime during that time.

Do I have to register?

Yes, please contact ESK members or register online.

What is being served?

Sorry, but we do not publish the meals ahead of time. You are welcome to ask as soon as you arrive.

Can I bring the whole family?

Absolutely! From babies to grandparents, everyone is welcome here!
