Who We Are

Serving our community with compassion and dignity

Our Mission

At Empathy Soup Kitchen, we believe in serving our community with compassion and dignity. Our mission is to provide nutritious meals to those in need while fostering a sense of community and hope.

How We Operate

Empathy Soup Kitchen operates on weekends as efficiently as possible, with the support of volunteers, food donations and private contributions, and an extremely small and dedicated staff.

Our Commitment

We are dedicated to serving the people with a place of comfort and acceptance as well as delicious and nutritious food. We gratefully accept both food and financial donations, which are tax deductible.
